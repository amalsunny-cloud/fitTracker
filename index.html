    Guidelines

    • Warm-Up: Begin with 5-10 minutes of light cardio to increase blood flow to your muscles and reduce the risk of injury.
    • Progressive Overload: Gradually increase the weight, frequency, or number of repetitions in your strength training routine to continue making gains in muscle size and strength.
    • Form and Technique: Focus on maintaining proper form to prevent injuries and maximize the effectiveness of each exercise.
    • Rest and Recovery: Allow 48 hours of rest between working the same muscle group to facilitate recovery and muscle growth.
    • Balanced Routine: Incorporate exercises that target all major muscle groups to ensure balanced development and prevent muscle imbalances.
